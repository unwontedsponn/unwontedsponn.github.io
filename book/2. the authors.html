The Authors

Ben Spooner
Creator of Beginner To Composer In 14 Days

Ben is a multi-instrumentalist, composer and creative. He is a piano and drum kit tutor and examiner for Rock School & Yamaha across seven popular instruments. He previously studied MSc in Music & Sound for Film and Games at Hertfordshire University (distinction) and performed as a session musician with artists at The Roundhouse, Abbey Road, The Edinburgh Festival and other notable venues.

benspooner.co.uk

Hayley J C Spooner
Co-Writer

Hayley is a writer, singer-songwriter, pianist and violinist. She studied BA English Literature and Language at Oxford University and Songwriting at Pointblank Music School, and works today as a copywriter for brands and creative projects.
