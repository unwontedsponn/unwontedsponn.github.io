About

A little bit about me...

In 2022, following a long career running my own music related business I realised that it's time for a change and pursue a career that combines some of my favourite skills of problem solving and creativity. This lead me to the exciting and daunting transition into the world of tech. My pursuits have lead me through web development, data science and software engineering and I'm now looking to join a company where I can serve their vision and grow in this field.

Brief history of my tech skills:

My love for coding came out of the blue. I was studying for an MSc in Music and Sound, when I began teaching myself C# so I could add sounds Iâ€™d created into computer games, using the Unity Game Engine. I enjoyed the process so much that it sparked a huge thirst to explore the other exciting things that code can do. After dipping my toe into C++ and the Unreal engine, I took the decision to study Front-End Development in earnest, using professional resources like Codecademy, freeCodeCamp and more to up-skill. Brief history of my audio skills:

My career to date has been involved with all aspects of music and sound. For the last 15 years I've performed (Joy Crookes), toured (Edinburgh Festival), taught (ABRSM), examined (RSL), composed (Kuoni), scored, produced and treated audio for a range of content. Audio is a passion in my life and I'm proud of my expertise in almost all areas. Except perhaps singing. In 2021 I composed the music to an award-winning short film called When We Fell. It was presented at festivals worldwide, won three awards and was nominated for the Audience Award by the prestigious British Animation Awards. Today, I release music independently as the artists Sponn and Green and Pine.
