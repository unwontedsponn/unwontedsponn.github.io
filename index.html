About

A little bit about me...

Following a 15yr career as a business owner, offering music/audio services, I transitioned into pastures new. I up-skilled in web development before securing a place on a competitive Hyperion Dev Software Engineering & Data Science bootcamp, competing against 20,000 other applicants. I am now looking to join a company where I can add value to a dynamic team and grow in this field.

Brief history of my tech skills:

My love of coding came out of the blue. I was studying for an MSc in Music and Sound and began teaching myself C# so I could add sounds to computer games in the Unity Game Engine. I enjoyed the process so much that it sparked a whole new direction. I used professional resources like Codecademy, freeCodeCamp and more to immerse myself in my new passion. I have now built up a portfolio of projects including a portfolio website, a prototype for a digital piano education book and a retro arcade game platform - all created solo and from scratch.

SKILLS:
WebDev: HTML, CSS, Javascript and game programming with the HTML5 canvas
Data Science: Python, SQL

Brief history of my audio skills:

For the last 15 years I've performed (Joy Crookes), toured (Edinburgh Festival), taught (ABRSM), examined (RSL), composed (Kuoni), scored, produced and treated audio for a range of content. In 2021 I composed the music to an award-winning short film called When We Fell. It was presented at festivals worldwide, won three awards and was nominated for the Audience Award by the prestigious British Animation Awards. Today, I release music independently as the artists Sponn and Green and Pine.
